Rules

24-golfer team format

Two teams of twelve play six two-man pairing matches using Net Better Ball Stroke Play.

2 teams of 12

Team White and Team Blue are selected during the live draft.

6 two-man matches

Each matchup has one two-man pairing from Team White and one two-man pairing from Team Blue.

Net Better Ball Stroke Play

Each side counts the better net score from its two golfers for the match.

1 point per match

A match win is worth 1 point. A tied match is worth 0.5 points to each team.
